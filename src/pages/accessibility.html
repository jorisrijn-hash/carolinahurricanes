At the arena

Lenovo Center

Sensory room

A quiet space on the main concourse with adjustable lighting, seating and noise cancelling headphones available to borrow at guest services.

Wheelchair and companion seating

Available in every price tier, not only the upper bowl. Book by phone or through the accessible seating filter on the ticket page.

Assistive listening

Receivers at guest services, no deposit. The arena bowl is fitted with a hearing loop.

Captioning

All in-bowl announcements and video board content are captioned on the ribbon boards.

Service animals

Welcome throughout the building. Relief areas are outside gates 1 and 3.

This website

How we build

Every page is keyboard operable, has a single main heading, visible focus outlines and text that meets contrast at level AA. Animation respects your system motion setting, and you can override it in settings.

Found something broken? Tell us and we will fix it, then say so here.

Report a barrier
Known gaps

Video clips in the play by play do not yet have captions. The seating map is navigable by keyboard through the tier buttons but individual seats are not yet selectable without a pointer.

Standards

We target WCAG 2.1 level AA. Contrast is verified against the palette on every build rather than checked by eye.

Data saver

Settings has a data saver mode that stops all background animation and heavy imagery. It is the fastest way to make the site usable on a weak connection.
